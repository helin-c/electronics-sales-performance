# Electronics Retail Store: Q1 2025 Sales Performance

## 📊 Project Overview
This project was completed as an individual technical task. The scenario involves acting as a Junior Data Analyst for a small retail store that sells electronics. The objective was to analyze sales data from the first quarter of 2025 and build an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize performance, track revenue trends, and identify key drivers of sales.

![Sales Performance Dashboard](https://github.com/helin-c/electronics-sales-performance/blob/main/electronics%20sales%20performance.png?raw=true)

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques
* **Power BI Desktop:** Used for data ingestion, visualization, and dashboard design.
* **Interactive Elements:** Implemented slicers and filters to allow users to drill down by Product Category, Store location, and Month.
* **Data Visualization:** Utilized KPIs, Bar Charts, Column Charts, and Line Charts to clearly communicate business metrics.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

### 1. Total Q1 Revenue
**Question:** What is the total revenue for Q1 2025?
* **Insight:** The total revenue generated for the first quarter of 2025 is **£785.37K**.

### 2. Top Performing Categories
**Question:** Which product category generated the most revenue?
* **Insight:** The **Laptops** category generated the highest revenue, bringing in £331K. This was followed by Phones (£250K), Tablets (£196K), and Accessories (£9K).

### 3. Store Volume Performance
**Question:** Which store sold the highest number of units?
* **Insight:** **Store B** sold the highest volume with a total of 445 units, narrowly beating Store C (408 units) and Store A (392 units).

### 4. Best-Selling Products
**Question:** What were the top 3 best-selling products by units sold?
* **Insight:** The top three products by volume were the **Galaxy Tab** (149 units), followed closely by the **Charger** (136 units) and the **Samsung S23** (130 units).

### 5. Quarterly Revenue Trend
**Question:** What was the trend of total revenue over the three months?
* **Insight:** Total revenue fluctuated significantly over the first quarter. It started at £188,025 in January, peaked sharply in February at £347,780, and then decreased to £249,560 in March.
